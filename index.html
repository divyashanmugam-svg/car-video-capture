Step before video

Locate engine and chassis number

Soft guide

Engine number

Usually stamped on the engine block inside the bonnet. Use the torch if the bay is dim.

  • Open the bonnet safely.
  • Look near the engine block or on a fixed metal surface.
  • Clean dust gently if the stamping is hard to read.

Chassis number

Often visible near the windshield base, the driver-side door pillar, or on a label in the engine bay.

  • Check the lower windshield corner first.
  • Then check the driver-side door jamb or B-pillar.
  • If needed, look for a VIN/chassis label under the bonnet.
